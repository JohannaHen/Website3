The person I am

My name is Johanna, I am Swedish and I am 23 years old.

I was born in Stockholm and I grew up in Hong Kong until the age of 15 and then i moved to Germany which is where i finished my last years of high school. I completed my bachelor studies in the UK at University of Bath and I have been living in London since then.

Growing up I did a lot of sports in my free time, at a young age I did a lot of swim training and later enjoyed running, both cross country and track. Today my favorite hobby is to sail in Sweden along the westcoast with friends and family.
