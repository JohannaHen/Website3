The person I am

My name is Johanna Henriksson, I am 23 years old and Swedish. I was born in Stockholm, however I moved with my family to Hong Kong at age three which is where I grew up. I later moved to Germany and finished high school in Frankfurt before starting my bachelors at University of Bath in the UK. Growing up I loved sports, mostly swimming and runnning (both cross country and track). When my studies began getting more serious I stopped competing, however I still enjoy sports in my free time for fun. During my free time I also like to see my friends who mostly live in London, and my family who are based in Germany and Sweden. .
